Dryland Triathlon

Roll. Ride. Run.

Saturday, October 25th, 2025 • Carver Park Reserve, Parley Lake Area

About the Event

Join us for TCSC's pilot year of the Dryland Triathlon! This fun event combines three common off-season activities for Nordic skiers: rollerskiing, mountain biking, and trail running. Show off your off-season gains before winter arrives!

Compete individually or with two teammates. Each leg starts and ends in the Parley Lake lot where your team will have a designated transition zone.

Distances & Schedule

Long Course 18K roll • 17K ride • 11K run 9:00 AM start
Short Course 9.5K roll • 9K ride • 6K run 9:30 AM start
