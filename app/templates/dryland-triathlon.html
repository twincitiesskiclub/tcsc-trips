Dryland Triathlon

Roll. Ride. Run.

Saturday, October 25th, 2025 – Carver Park Reserve, Parley Lake Area

About the Event

Join us for TCSC's pilot year of the Dryland Triathlon! This fun event combines three common off-season activities for Nordic skiers: rollerskiing, mountain biking, and trail running. Show off your off-season gains before winter arrives!

Compete individually or with two teammates. Each leg starts and ends in the Parley Lake lot where your team will have a designated transition zone.

Distances & Schedule

  • Long course: 18K roll, 17K ride, 11K run – starts at 9:00 AM
  • Short course: 9.5K roll, 9K ride, 6K run – starts at 9:30 AM

Equipment Rules

  • Roll: Fastest wheels allowed are #2 or Marwe #6. Freestyle technique only.
  • Ride: No e-bikes. Rigid, hardtail, or full-suspension mountain bikes are welcome.
  • Helmets required for all legs.

Registration

Registration opens July 25th, 2025 and is limited to 100 total participants or teams (50 short course, 50 long course). Pricing will be announced soon.

About TCSC

The Twin Cities Ski Club (TCSC) is a welcoming community of Nordic skiers offering training, trips, and fun events for skiers of all abilities. Come meet fellow skiers and join the community!
