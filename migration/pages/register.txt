Seasons
Spring & Summer
Training sessions are held on Tuesdays and Thursdays from 6:15PM - 8:15PM from mid May through August with a 2 week "4th of July Break". We try to jump around to parks and trails all over the metro, but Theodore Wirth and Hyland are two mainstays in our rotation. Our training program aims to develop ski-specific skills and endurance for the upcoming season. Skiers can expect dryland sessions focusing on endurance, strength, agility, and technique work.
Fall & Winter
Training ses
sions are held on Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday from 6:15PM - 8:15PM between September and March at various Twin Cities locations. Our training program aims to develop ski-specific skills and endurance for the upcoming season.
